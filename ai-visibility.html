How AI actually answers

One question becomes a dozen behind the screen.

When a buyer asks "who's the best in Ljubljana for X," the engine doesn't run one search. It dispatches a fan-out across the live web, retrieves pages for each sub-question, throws most away, and synthesizes what survives. A page that answers one sub-question wins one slice and loses the rest.

"best custom home builder near me"
↓ fans out into ↓
what should a custom build cost
how long do permits take
what is the process
who handles ADUs
energy-efficient builders
warranty & aftercare
real client reviews
typical timeline
which neighborhoods
pricing transparency

Whoever answers the most of the fan-out, in the most credible way, gets cited the most.

The method

Four signals AI cross-checks before it trusts your brand.

LLMs don't rank single pages — they look for the same story repeated across different kinds of sources. This is called triangulation. Echo chambers (250 identical posts on your own site) get filtered. Authority comes from a diverse footprint:

Bucket 01

Owned content

Data-driven case studies on your site. Real pricing, timeframes, processes — the things competitors can copy from your homepage in an afternoon, but cannot copy from your data.

"Replaced 14 water heaters in Miller Beach — cut install time by 2h via a new staging process."
Bucket 02

Professional platforms

LinkedIn articles, guest posts, niche publications. When the AI sees your expertise on your site and on a third-party professional platform, it begins to verify you.

linkedin.com · industry trade press · YouTube
Bucket 03

Community content

Reddit, Quora and local forums — where AI looks for "real people talking." Helpful, specific advice in the right threads puts you where the model already trusts the room.

reddit.com/r/ljubljana · niche subreddits · Quora
Bucket 04

Third-party validation

Press mentions, local sponsorships, directories. Sources you don't write yourself — the final triangulation step that locks in the pattern.

local press · directories · partnerships
