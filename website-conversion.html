Where money leaks

Why a "nice looking" site still doesn't convert.

Most underperforming sites aren't ugly — they're confused. The visitor doesn't know who it's for, what it does, or what to do next within the first five seconds.

What kills the conversion

  • Vague headline that doesn't say who it's for
  • Slow load, layout shift, broken on mobile
  • Five different CTAs competing for attention
  • No proof — no reviews, results or named clients
  • Generic stock photos, zero personality

What I build instead

  • A headline a 12-year-old understands in 3 seconds
  • Sub-1s loads, mobile-first, zero layout jank
  • One primary CTA, repeated where it counts
  • Proof placed exactly where doubt appears
  • A design that looks like you, not a template
